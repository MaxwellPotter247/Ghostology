Ghostology Stories: A Paranormal Podcast

In this podcast, author Scott V Brian recounts some true life ghost stories he has encountered in his decades of paranormal research. With soothing background tracks, this podcast will lure you in to connect with tales from what lies beyond. First episodes to be released August 2021 on Red Circle, to be available on Spotify, Apple Podcast and other major platforms.

Ghostology Talks: A Paranormal Podcast

In this podcast, SEIPO investigators Scott V Brian and Kevin Bowerman team up to discuss some of their most intriguing investigations, along with other occasional guest experts from the field. They will break down the details of their ghostly encounters, discuss possible theories to debunk what they experienced, and leave you with interesting questions to ponder.
